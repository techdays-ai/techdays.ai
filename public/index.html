What it is

Every lesson is built from executable materials.

Each lesson is assembled from three working materials. Nothing on the page is an illustration of the idea — it is the idea, running.

Runnable playbooks

Step-by-step workflows for real tasks — drafting, analysis, agent orchestration — where every step executes inside the lesson.

step 3/7 triage the inbox
▸ ran in lesson — 42 messages sorted
step 4/7 draft the escalation

An always-on tutor

A tutor woven into each micro-interaction, not a separate chat box. It reads where a learner is stuck and responds in context.

tutor — your prompt lost the constraint
from step 2. Want to see the diff, or
try once more on your own?

Templates you run in place

A prompt library scoped to the business. Learners fill in the variables, run them against live models, and keep what works.

brief = template("weekly-rollup")
brief.team = "underwriting"
▸ ran — saved to your library

The curriculum

One arc, four stages: first prompt to production agent.

The stages are the path. Learners enter where they are and move one stage further, with work they can show at the end of each.

stage 01

First prompt

for every professional

Ends able toFrame any task as a prompt with context, constraints, and a checkable result.

stage 02

Working fluency

for teams and their leads

Ends able toRun daily work through playbooks and a shared prompt library — and improve both.

stage 03

Agents & skills

for builders and power users

Ends able toCompose agents, skills, and tools into workflows that hold up against real data. ▸ open this lesson

stage 04

Production agents

for engineering teams

Ends able toOperationalize agentic systems — A2A, MCP, evals, guardrails — at enterprise scale.

What's included

What a learner has at the end.

Each stage ends in something demonstrable — hands on keyboard, on tasks from the actual business rather than quizzes.

  • ✓Shipped artifacts — prompts, playbooks, and agents in use on the team, not certificates.
  • ✓Team prompt libraries, versioned and shared across the org, that outlast the workshop.
  • ✓Capability assessments before and after, measured on real tasks from the business.
  • ✓A skills hub teams keep building on, from first prompt to production agent.
