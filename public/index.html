How it works

Not another video library. A room where the ideas run.

Every lesson is built from three working materials. Nothing on the page is an illustration of the thing — it is the thing.

Step-by-step workflows for real tasks — drafting, analysis, agent orchestration — where every step executes right in the lesson.

The curriculum

One arc, four stages: first prompt to production agent.

The numbers are the path. Every learner enters where they are and leaves one stage further, with work they can show.

stage 01

First prompt

for every professional

Walks out able toFrame any task as a prompt with context, constraints, and a checkable result.

stage 02

Working fluency

for teams and their leads

Walks out able toRun daily work through playbooks and a shared prompt library — and improve both.

stage 03

Agents & skills

for builders and power users

Walks out able toCompose agents, skills, and tools into workflows that survive contact with real data.

stage 04

Production agents

for engineering teams
